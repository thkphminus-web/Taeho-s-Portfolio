# Published Default Video Assets

Place your default video files here in the `public` directory so they are compiled as public assets and shown by default to any visitor of your published application.

## Expected Files:
- **System 01 - Mechatronic System Integration**: Name your video `video_mechatronic.mp4` and place it here.
- **System 02 - TinyML on-device learning & BP monitoring**: Name your video `video_tinyml.mp4` and place it here.
- **System 03 - Soft Robotics & Wearable Bio-devices**: Name your video `video_soft_robotics.mp4` and place it here.
- **System 04 - Resilient Robotic Architectures**: Name your video `video_resilient_robotics.mp4` and place it here.

For example, if you upload `video_tinyml.mp4` here, the application will automatically play it by default for everyone!
